    Polyjuice Potion

  • Properties

    Polyjuice potion is a complicated elixir that even skilled witches and wizards have trouble brewing. If made correctly, it allows the drinker to assume another personâ€™s appearance for 1 hour, regardless of differences in age or gender

  • Warning

    Used for changing into other human appearances ONLY. If animal DNA is used, seek medical attention immediately!

  • Ingredients

    You will need the following ingredients to brew this potion:

    • Fluxweed
    • Knotgrass
    • Lacewing flies
    • Leeches
    • Bicorn horn
    • Boomslang skin
    • Bit of person you want to turn into (hair, fingernail, etc)


    *Note - Fluxweed and knotgrass is similar in appearance, but fluxweed is a lighter green.
