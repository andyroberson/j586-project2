• How to Brew

    Polyjuice Potion

  • Properties

    Polyjuice potion is a complicated elixir that even skilled witches and wizards have trouble brewing. If made correctly, it allows the drinker to assume another personâ€™s appearance for 1 hour, regardless of differences in age or gender

  • Warning

    Used for changing into other human appearances ONLY. If animal DNA is used, seek medical attention immediately!

  • Ingredients


    You will need the following ingredients to brew this potion:

    • Fluxweed
    • Knotgrass
    • Lacewing flies
    • Leeches
    • Bicorn horn
    • Boomslang skin
    • Bit of person you want to turn into (hair, fingernail, etc)


    *Note - Fluxweed and knotgrass are similar in appearance, but fluxweed is a lighter green.

  • How to Brew - Part 1


    Brewing polyjuice potion is a complicated process. Make sure to follow directions!

    1. 1. Add 3 bundles of fluxweed to the cauldron.
    2. 2. Add 2 bundles of knotgrass to the cauldron.
    3. 3. Stir 3 times, clockwise.
    4. 4. Wave your wand over the cauldron.
    5. 5. Leave to brew and return in 60 minutes
    6. 6. Add 4 leeches to the cauldron.
    7. 7. Add 1 cup of lacewings to the mortar.
    8. 8. Crush to a fine powder.
    9. 9. Add 2 cups of crushed lacewings to the cauldron.
    10. 10. Heat mixture for 30 seconds on a low heat.
    11. 11. Wave your wand over the cauldron to complete part 1.

    You must wait at least 18 hours before continuing to part 2

  • How to Brew - Part 2


    1. 1. Add 3 cups of shredded boomslang skin to the cauldron.
    2. 2. Add 1 cup of bicorn horn to the mortar.
    3. 3. Crush to a fine powder.
    4. 4. Add 1 cup of a crushed bicorn horn to your cauldron.
    5. 5. Heat for 20 seconds on a high heat.
    6. 6. Wave your wand over the cauldron.
    7. 7. Leave to brew and return in 25 hours.
    8. 8. Add 1 tablespoon of lacewings to the cauldron.
    9. 9. Stir 3 times anti-clockwise.
    10. 10. Add bit of the person to the cauldron.
    11. 11. Wave your wand to complete the potion!
